# Curriculum — NeetCode 150 (Java)

The **official NeetCode 150**. Covers every pattern that appears in FAANG interviews.
Extra breadth (250/300) is omitted per the Oct 2026 plan re-scope — we want
**depth over breadth**: 4 passes on 150 beats 3 passes on 300.

Format: `#` · `LC ID` · `Title` · `Difficulty` (E/M/H).
